Quality Education

The institute strives to set high standards of education by adopting industry-endorsed syllabus updated from time to time and ensuring effective learning through the case method of teaching, flipped class as well as experiential and immersive learning.

Focused Training

The institute empowers the students and makes them corporate ready through various modules on communication and soft skills, reasoning, quantitative aptitude, current affairs, business awareness, and analytical deliberations on various corporate sectors.

Assured Placements

The institute maintains good rapport with various industries across various sectors. Placement is assured to all students of MBA and IMBA with good Placement Priority Score (PPS).

Carving a legacy since 18 years, Srusti Academy of Management today is one of the leading Management institutes in Odisha. It has been steadily ranked higher amongst the B-Schools by various credible ranking agencies. The Institute Accredited as “A” Grade Institute by National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC) since 2003 has been recently, has been recently ranked 147th in India and 15th in eastern India by leading Magazine “The Week” in the B-School ranking 2021.An institutional life member of All India Management Association (AIMA), Association of Indian Management School (AIMS) and member in professional bodies like CMAI and NHRD. Srusti Academy of Management on conferment of autonomy by Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT), Odisha, Rourkela on recommendation of UGC as per provisions of clause 3.13 and clause 6.4(i) of UGC regulations dated 12.02.2018 is now adopting updated industry endorsed syllabus with learning emphasis and focused training on soft skill, reasoning and quantitative aptitude, IT skill etc. to prepare students for most challenging job roles in corporate. Students are also encouraged to inculcate professional ethics and values. The institute has been able to consistently create multi-dimensional experiences for students that transform them into becoming professional assets.

Advantages of Srusti Academy of Management

In order to make business education more relevant and to create “Industry fit” professionals, the institute after conferment of autonomy has updated the syllabus of MBA and IMBA by introducing data analytics in all the core subjects, HR interventions like competence mapping, assessment center in HR area and Financial modelling in view the changes happening in corporate management.

The Training and Placement cell carefully evaluates students and maps the knowledge and skill gap and accordingly groups the students and designs the training inputs with varied focus and intensity with the objective of improvement of students to be successful during campus recruitment process. Emphasis is given on presentation; group discussion (GD) CV based personal interview and SPP presentations.

Alumni of Srusti richly contribute to the all round development of their alma mater. Registered in 2004 under the societies registration act 1860, Srusti Alumni Association has its elected body of office bearers with President of the association at helm of affairs. Members of alumni association working in various corporate houses in India and abroad share their experience among the juniors. The association, through its chapters in important cities like Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai and Delhi-NCR maintains a close networking among all “Srustians” for upliftment of their alma mater

Faculty Members

Our faculty team comprises of academicians as well as industry professionals who come with great deal of expertise in their respective fields.Faculty members not only provide highest quality classroom instruction toward the attainment of the course outcome and programme outcome, but also guide students through their assignments and project works. They are also actively engaged in research in their respective core competencies with support from affiliating University ,AICTE and UGC.
